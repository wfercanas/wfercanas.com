I understand that frequently our minds conceive new product ideas that we believe can be successful. Products that solve problems we think are meaningful, so the only thing that is left to do is to just start creating them.

The idea may get boosted quickly if you have the chance to gather a team soon to start building. Stop. Let’s think a little more before jumping in.

## Let’s Check If Someone Desires It

Perhaps the problem we got at hand is relevant only for us. Perhaps it is important for many, but they can live perfectly without our solution.

Before beginning to build anything, we should drink a lot of coffees. Coffees with people, coffees with friends, colleagues and unknown persons. People with whom we can talk about the problem and why it matters to solve it.

It is importat to talk and see how vibrant is the conversation.

If we don’t get to move the people that live with intensity the problems we aim to solve, maybe is better to leave the idea right there. At the end, although the decision of using our products may be rational, it is in a greater degree an emotional one.

## Stick It On The Wall

When I was a kid I used to dream about becoming a professional soccer player. Many of us did. Almost all of us sticked a poster of our favorite player on the wall.

When you do this, you are no just sticking a piece of paper or a photo. You are sticking a dream.

When you wake up you see it, when you play with your friends you make pretend your are him/her. When you see them in TV you admire them, when they score you celebrate, when they fall you cry. You look at their details, their passes, their way of running, jumping and dodging.

I believe we should do the same with our product ideas. After so many coffees, we should have a flag to raise. A poster to stick on the wall. You are not building anything yet, but you do dedicate time to visualize the product with their compelling details.

We need a poster to see what we want to become. Let’s build the poster of our product and stick it on the wall. Visualize your dream and achieve it.

## Lead, Don’t Dictate

There is no way in which you can achieve the dream on your own. It doesn’t work that all the decisions that enable a product to grow and be successful flow only from you. It is also useless that you decide to save all the tough decisions for yourself.

Gathering all the power about what can and cannot be done with the product will only leave you as a lone dreamer. If you share the vision, lead, assist the process, advise and delegate, you will create the room for other people to dream what you dream. Only if your team dreams intensively as you, you will succeed.

## Gather The Team

If you are ready to do this, gather your team and conquer. Remember, even if you start building your dream, **never stop drinking coffees**.
